About

I'm a web developer based in Córdoba, with formal training in Web Application Development (DAW) and hands-on experience deploying and configuring software in real industrial environments.

My current role involves working on MOM/MES systems for industrial digitalisation projects — configuring manufacturing software, integrating data from OPC and SQL sources, and doing functional testing in production environments. It's not typical frontend work, but it's taught me that software has to actually work, not just look like it does.

What I want to do next is focus on the web side: building interfaces, products, and applications that people use. I'm interested in frontend development, product thinking, and the kind of work that happens at good digital agencies and tech companies.

I learn fast, I take code quality seriously, and I'm at the stage where I want to go from building things alone to building things with a team.
